Anthony's Automated Feature
Who is this device for? People with a physical disability or those who buy a lot of things in bulk or large items.
What problem does it solve?
  • Needing to hold and close the door manually.
  • Having to carry keys with you
What items do you need?
  • 2 microbits
  • 2 Breakoutboards
  • 4 Push buttons
  • 2 Servo motors
  • 6 Wires
  • 8 Alligator clips with pig tail or male to female jumper wires
  • 1 Breadboard
Protype images
What are the Negative Effects of Computer Device Disposal? When metals like lead, mercury, and cadmium are placed in a landfill they can leak into the soil, which can kill crops and contaminate nearby water sources.
Water Pollution
  • Metals can be eaten up by fish and can add up along the food chain
  • We can't eat the fish because of the bioaccumulation
  • Chemicals from e-waste disrupt the aquatic ecosystems
Disturbance to Farmers
  • Changes the soil's chemical compound
  • The soil is less fertile
  • Less crop yield
  • Heavy metals build up in animals and insects that help with crop growth
